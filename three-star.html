The Charm Offensive by Alison Cochrum

Pages: 368

Dates read: Mar 15th to 19th

★ ★ ★ ☆ ☆

This is what me and my cousin call an “Oreo book.” It’s easy and good, but an Oreo doesn’t feel like a cookie, and Oreo books don’t feel like reading a book. An easy good choice, but not the real deal.
